Favorite City: San Francisco

San Francisco is my favorite city because of its important iconic landmarks and vibrant culture. The Golden Gate Bridge and Alcatraz Island are just a couple of the city's many historic sites.The blend of diverse neighborhoods and stunning waterfront views makes it a unique and enchanting place to visit.
